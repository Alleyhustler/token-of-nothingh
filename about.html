about me

Hello! My name is cc (pronounced see-see, spelt in lowercase please and thank you).

I live in Australia. You can use whatever pronouns you want to refer to me. I'm a queer, mixed-race marxist.

The fandoms I've contributed to so far are: Team Fortress 2, Yakuza/Like a Dragon and Alan Wake II. But I have a whole lot of other interests too!

  • I'm a big proponent of hobbyist culture: making things just for fun and not for money or clout.
  • I'm autistic! My review of being autistic as an adult: not too bad.
